    As a final step in this project, I wrote a CER to answer the main question, "Is race biologically real?"

    Claim

    Race is not biologically real.

    Evidence

    According to our research and testing, people matched DNA with others without the same race/ethnicity proving race does not determine everything from a biological perspective. For example, in my DNA matching group, Me(Latina/Black), Laurel(White), Noor(east Asian), Lukas(Latino), Obinna(African-american) , Mehreen(Middle Eastern), and Raihana(Middle Eastern) all fell in the same group regardless of all of us not being from the same places or having the same background. Even though some of us match in skin tone, heritage, or even religion, we still are not the same, but yet fell in the same group.

    Reasoning Part 1

    Race is reliable from a biological perspective because race to me is the physical appearance of someone. For example skin tone, hair type, facial features, etc.

    Reasoning Part 2

    I know that race is not biologically because if it was it would contradict both my evidence and the evidence we see in our everyday lives.
